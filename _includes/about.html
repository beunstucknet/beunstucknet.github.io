About Unstuck


Unstuck is an all-in-one tool for managing online appointments. Here are some things you can do with Unstuck:

  • Offer you availability for with a simple booking page
  • Automatically remove times that conflict with events in your calendar
  • Automatically create Google Calendar (+ Google Meet) appointments with your clients
  • Securely invoice and collect payment using our integrated Stripe flow
  • Create multiple types of appointments
  • Create direct appointment offers to specific clients via email
Unstuck is free to try. We charge a flat 1% transaction fee for paid appointments which helps us cover our costs. No subscriptions, no hidden charges, no tricks to lock you in.
